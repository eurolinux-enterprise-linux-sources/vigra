Detailed Description

template<class T>
class vigra::RGB2XYZFunctor< T >

Convert linear (raw) RGB into standardized tri-stimulus XYZ.

#include <vigra/colorconversions.hxx>
Namespace: vigra

According to ITU-R Recommendation BT.709, the functor realizes the transformation

By default, . This default can be overridden in the constructor. X, Y, and Z are always positive and reach their maximum for white. The white point is obtained by transforming RGB(255, 255, 255). It corresponds to the D65 illuminant. Y represents the luminance ("brightness") of the color.
